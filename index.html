About Us

Revival Life Church is a place where anyone can come to enounter the presence of God. As in our video above, we hold dear to worship, serving and community. We are a Holy Spirit-filled, Christ centered, Bible-believing church who carry out the Great Commission found in Matthew 28:19-20, "Therefore go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, and teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you. And surely I am with you always, to the very end of the age.”
